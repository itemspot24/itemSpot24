Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how iTemSpot ("the App") handles your information.

Information We Collect

iTemSpot is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Stored Locally

  • Item names, notes, tags, and photos you create
  • Location names and hierarchies
  • AR world map data (device-specific, never synced)
  • App preferences (appearance, font size, language)

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your items and locations sync across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your private iCloud database and is accessible only to you. AR world map data is never synced and remains on the device where it was created.

What We Don't Collect

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No personal information transmitted to external servers

Camera & Photo Access

The App requests camera access for AR room scanning, QR code scanning, and item photography. Photo library access is requested for saving QR codes and importing item photos. These permissions are requested only when needed and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

iTemSpot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
